1. Why should this be a co-founder role instead of hiring a Head of Growth? What makes this role require equity?

   Notes:

2. Why me? What specifically does Nam see in me that he cannot find in someone else? How many other candidates has he talked to, and why has he not closed with anyone yet?

3. What is the specific scope of this role? Pricing, positioning, budget, hiring, market selection, marketing, sales, customer relations, investor relations, etc. What am I not allowed to decide?

4. Where is the boundary with Nam? Who makes the final decision when the two sides disagree on go-to-market?
